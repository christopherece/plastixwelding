About Huapai Plastic Welding & Repairs

Huapai Plastic Welding & Repairs is a family-owned business based in Kumeu, Auckland, offering high-quality plastic welding and repair services. Whether it’s a cracked bumper, broken pipe, or shattered plastic part, we bring it back to life with precision and care.

We also offer sustainable, cost-effective solutions — from farm equipment to water tanks and more. No job is too big or too small: Don’t replace it – let us fix it!
