About the Business

At Huapai Plastic Welding and Repairs, we specialize in high-quality plastic and fibreglass repairs for a wide range of items — from motorcycle fairings and car bumpers to spa pools, kayaks, and more. With years of hands-on experience and a passion for bringing things back to life, we take pride in offering reliable, cost-effective solutions that help our customers avoid expensive replacements. Whether it’s a cracked water tank, a damaged boat hull, or a broken fuel tank, we’ve got the tools and expertise to get it fixed — quickly and properly. We provide both mobile repair services and in-house repairs, giving you flexibility and convenience no matter where you are. Our motto is simple: "If it’s plastic, I can fix it!"
