Our Services

Water Tank Repairs

We restore damaged or leaking water tanks, extending their lifespan and saving you the cost of replacement. Our repairs are durable, reliable, and safe for potable water use.

Tote & Fuel Tank Repairs

From minor cracks to structural splits, we expertly repair tote and fuel tanks to meet safety and performance needs across agricultural, industrial, and commercial use.

Plastic Crate Repairs

Whether used for storage, transport, or logistics, we repair cracked or broken plastic crates quickly and effectively, helping your operations stay efficient and cost-effective.

Shower Tray Repairs

Cracked or leaking shower trays can lead to costly water damage. Our seamless plastic welding process restores them to like-new condition with minimal disruption.

Electrofusion Welding

We provide electrofusion welding to create strong, leak-proof joints by using a specialized machine that heats and fuses plastic pipes together. This reliable service is ideal for water, gas, and industrial piping systems, ensuring long-lasting and secure connections for your projects.
